Lambda DevDesk

Students at Lambda School need a place where they can escalate their concerns and receive help. This app will allow an admin to manage help desk tickets that come in from Lambda School Students. It also allows students to submit a help desk ticket, categorize it and post it to the help channel.

How we can Help

Lambda DevDesk will solve the following pain points when needing to track and organize issue that students need resolved.

Submit.

Students are able to login to create and submit a new help desk ticket, and are able to see all tickets that are currently open for help!

Categorize.

Lambda help desk tickets are categorized based on the title, description, "what I've tried", and category that the student included when creating their ticket!

Organize.

An admin or helper can see all the open tickets, can assign a ticket to themselves, and can resolve tickets that they have resolved!

The days of long forgotten or unheard student issues can be gone by using Lambda DevDesk to track and organize issues that students need help with!
